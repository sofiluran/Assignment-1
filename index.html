Welcome to Skål Stockholm!

Welcome to your insider's guide to the city's most unforgettable bars. Whether you're in the mood for a rooftop cocktail with skyline views, a cozy pub tucked away in a cobbled alley, or a buzzing craft beer haven, we've got you covered. We explore every corner of Stockholm — from the historic charm of Gamla Stan to the hip hideouts of Södermalm — to bring you the best places to drink, mingle, and soak up the local vibe. Skål Stockholm helps you drink like a local and discover the soul of the city, one pint at a time.
